ABOUT

Alexander Lux

For years Alexander Lux is an established artistic photographer.

The high demands on his work can be seen in the variety of motifs, techniques used and the exceptional implementation.

The range of his work includes the presentation of supposed trivialities to large projects.

Here Alexander Lux submits to his motives and gives them his characteristic signature.

His work impressed and inspired among others Mothers Finest, Peter Maffay band the Panikorchester & Udo Lindenberg and many more.
